Fluid Simulation

This program simulates particles moving inside a closed box where they can effect eachother only within a certain range the force of this effect is more pronounced the closer the particles are to eachother, you can see the total effect in an area by looking at the colour of the pixels at that point. Red means that the "pressure" is greater than the average, and blue means the "pressure" is less than the average. It is also black if there are no particles effecting that region at all. You can reduce the area of effect and increase the gravity to more closely simulate liquids as well as decrease the gravity and increase the area of effect to more closely simulate gasses.
